# Snipe-IT Chat

Photograph an item or ask a question about your inventory.

**Add a new item**: attach a photo and say "add this to inventory". The assistant will identify the item, search for matching manufacturers/models, and ask for anything it can't determine from the photo (asset tag, location, serial number) before creating the record.

**Query inventory**: ask things like "list the first 10 assets", "how many laptops do we have?", or "what's assigned to jane@example.com?".

All tool calls are visible as collapsible steps so you can see exactly what the assistant did in Snipe-IT.
